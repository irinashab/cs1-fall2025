Required Materials

The required textbook is Learning Processing: A Beginner's Guide to Programming Images, Animation, and Interaction (2nd Edition, The Morgan Kaufmann Series in Computer Graphics) by Dan Shiffman.
It is available through the Purchase College Library, licensed for unlimited simultaneous users.
Simply click the link, sign in with your Purchase credentials, and read online—or download it for offline use.

Occasionally, you may see the error “This site does not provide a secure connection.”
Possible fixes:

  • Try a different browser
  • Clear your cache/history (choose “all time”)
  • Use an incognito or private window
If the issue continues, please contact me and we’ll sort it out together.
